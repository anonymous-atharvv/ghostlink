### 6.1 Our Technical Inability to Assist

GhostLink uses the Signal Protocol for end-to-end encryption. This means:

- We technically cannot access the plaintext content of your messages
- We cannot comply with law enforcement requests for message content — not by choice, but because we do not possess the decryption keys
- The decryption keys exist only on your device

### 6.2 What We Can Provide

In response to lawful legal process, we may be required to provide:
- Your username
- Account creation date
- Last connection timestamp
- Group membership (account IDs)

We cannot provide your IP address (not logged), your real identity (not collected), or your message content (technically impossible).

### 6.3 Use at Your Own Risk

While we have designed GhostLink to maximize privacy, no system is perfect. You are responsible for understanding:
- Device security (a compromised device can expose decrypted messages)
- Operational security (your recipient's device may not be secure)
